Family:

The Zap is a family mixed from an African bird called Hamerkop bird which has a special power to support 100 times over their weight protecting their adult bringing their nests. Also, the Zap is a mix of a Painted Frogfish from the deep sea that has a characteristic to camouflage. The Zap is called a species of Scopus Pictus because one time a Hammerkop bird was trying to get a food dipping inside of the sea and ate the painted frogfish. Eventually, the Hamerkop bird's DNA was poised from the Painted Frogfish, blended a new-mixed family from Anteariidae and Umbretta family and flew to sea space. At last, the species is called Scopus Pictus which became a creature that flies, protects, and camouflages in the sea space.

Habitat:

The Zaps can be either an eater of flame in Planet Octa, plasma in Planet Victa, wind in Planet Mucto, and thunder in Planet Zecto. Whenever the Zap passes through the planets, the Zap is sending energy to make the planets live.
